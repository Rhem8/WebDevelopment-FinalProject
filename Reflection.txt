# Reflection

The National Teachers College aims to develop students with good character and skills that can help the society.

Doing this project taught me patience, responsibility, and hard work.
There were many mistakes and problems while coding, but I kept practicing and fixing them until the project worked properly. 
This experience helped me become more disciplined and motivated to learn.

Since technology is becoming more important in the Philippines, many schools, businesses, and services now depend on websites and online systems.
By learning web development, I gained skills that can be useful in the future and can help create better digital solutions for people.

This project shows my effort, learning, and improvement in web development. 
It may not be perfect, but it reflects the values of responsibility, perseverance, and continuous learning that the NTC encourages in its students.
